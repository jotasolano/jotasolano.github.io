Our roads kill

A visualization of deaths on US roads in 2015

In 2015 there were 35.092 reported fatalities
on US roads and highways

This is the daily view of fatalities. At least 18
and up to 92 on a day

The monthly view of fatalities shows a
peak in August

The fatalities asociated with drunk drivers
account for 25% of all deaths

Surprisingly, most incidents ocurred
during clear weather conditions
